What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the art of protecting networks, devices, and data from unauthorized access or criminal use. It's about being one step ahead of digital threats and safeguarding what matters most in the information age.

Top Cyber Threats

Phishing Attacks

Fake emails or websites tricking you into giving up sensitive info.

Ransomware

Malware that locks your data and demands payment to unlock it.

Zero-Day Exploits

Attacks using previously unknown vulnerabilities in software.
